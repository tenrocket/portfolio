ABOUT ME

I believe in bravery. Sure I love making things, but I became a developer because I used to say it was impossible. I sold myself excuses like, "I'm too right-brained to be an engineer", and "That's just not how my mind works". I'm no fan of self-imposed limitations, and my work is a reflection of my lifelong quest to build a more empowered world. As you look through my portfolio, I hope it inspires you to strive for excellence in your own life. Hopefully you will notice an eye for design as well.
